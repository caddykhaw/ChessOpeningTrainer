About Chessology!

Welcome to Chessology!, your friendly chess opening trainer designed to help you master the art of chess openings. Created by Caddy Khaw in 2025, this tool aims to make learning chess fun, interactive, and accessible for players of all levels.

Whether you're a beginner looking to grasp the basics or an advanced player refining your repertoire, Chessology! offers a curated selection of the most popular white and black openings. With a sleek interface, move-by-move guidance, and a dark mode for late-night study sessions, we’ve got you covered.

We’re passionate about growing the chess community and are seeking funding and investment to take Chessology! to the next level. Part of any support we receive will be donated or directly used to organize chess training programs for individuals with disabilities in Malaysia, including the blind and others, making chess accessible to all through touch, sound, and more. For those who rely on touch or face other challenges and wish to get involved, please contact me at chessology@gmail.com. This project is a labor of love, built with passion and a bit of caffeine—join us in making chess more inclusive and exciting for everyone!
